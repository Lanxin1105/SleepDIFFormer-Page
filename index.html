Abstract

SleepDIFFormer is a transformer-based framework designed for multivariate sleep signal modeling across multiple modalities. It introduces differential attention mechanisms and domain generalization strategies to enable robust and accurate sleep stage classification across diverse datasets. We evaluate our model on five public datasets, achieving competitive performance under cross-dataset settings.
